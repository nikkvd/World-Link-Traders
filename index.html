About Us

World Link traders was officially established on January 03, 2008, domiciled in New Delhi, India. The company is engaged in the export of Premium grade Nilambur plantation teak wood sawn sizes,Local teak wood sawn sizes, customised sizes for individual use, other wood species like Rose wood, Mahagony, Jack fruit wood, Wild jack fruit wood, Rubber wood etc as sawn sizes and readymade premium doors and window frames as its main business.



The main raw material being Teak wood (Tectona grandis) and the main market share to European, American, Australian and Asian countries. In order to maintain buyer confidence, especially in terms of quality, World Link Traders prioritizes the quality of Teak wood raw materials and the quality of machine production in accordance with the company's vision.

Company Vision

Become a Creative, Innovative and Trusted Company.

Company Mission

Provide added value to products, Foster good relations with employees, Provide added value to the company.

Our Customers

Premium boat builders, furniture manufacturers ,construction companies ,indoor and out door decking manufacturers who really needs good quality wood.The most important factor is starting from the selection and handling of raw materials, especially at the level of dryness, so that when sent to buyers abroad the wood will not break or 'Crack' due to changes and differences in humidity and temperature levels from the country of origin and is not easily damaged.
